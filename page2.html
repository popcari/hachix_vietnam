    Greeting

    Technology needs to be available for everyone

    In recent years, the information industry has been evolving day by day. Artificial intelligence technology is transforming in various industries (medical, autonomous driving, automatic translation, etc.). Furthermore, the framework of Industrial 4.0 is steadily advancing by fusing IoT (Internet Of Things) and artificial intelligence technology.

    However, the current situation is that these technologies are still expensive and complicated. As a result, the benefits of recent technological innovations for general users and SMEs are not yet significant. HACHIX aims to realize a richer and more equal world by promptly providing the latest technology system and providing the benefits of the information revolution to general users.

    To fulfill that mission, we will continue to challenge unknown technologies and strengthen our ability to provide the best system solutions. We promise to be a partner who can think, worry and grow together with our customers, always listening to their voices, not just technology.
